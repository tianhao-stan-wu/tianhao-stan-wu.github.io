I received my bachelor's degrees in [Applied and Computational Mathematics](https://dornsife.usc.edu/mathematics/) and [Computer Science and Computer Engineering](https://www.cs.usc.edu/) from University of Southern California. Currently, I am doing MSCS at USC and plan to apply for PhD programs starting in 2027.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **formal methods, robotics, and machine learning**, with the goal of developing frameworks that leverage both the generalization capabilities of data-driven approaches and theoretical foundations of formal techiques. These frameworks aim to provide robust safety guarantees for systems operating in complex and uncertain environments. I am also interested in autonomous driving applications.
