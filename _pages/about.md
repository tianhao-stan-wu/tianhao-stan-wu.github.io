I received my bachelor's degrees in [Applied and Computational Mathematics](https://dornsife.usc.edu/mathematics/) and [Computer Science and Computer Engineering](https://www.cs.usc.edu/) from University of Southern California. I am currently continuing my studies in computer science at USC and will apply for PhD programs starting in 2027.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **formal methods, robotics, and machine learning**, with the goal of developing formal frameworks that integrate the generalizability of data-driven approaches with rigorous theoretical support to provide robust safety guarantees for systems that operate in complex and uncertain enviornments. Meanwhile, I am especially interested in deployment and verification of these frameworks in autonomous driving applications.
